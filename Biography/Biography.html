Holy Ghost! is an art collective stemming from the Chicago suburbs. Our brand was dreamed up and designed by teenagers who enjoy hanging out and doing dumb shit. We're making a platform for like-minded creatives to come together in order to produce content by the youth, for the youth. Expect clothes, music, and all kinds of art coming from us in the future.
